Fun With Voronoi Diagrams (VD)

Instructions (QAD): Click+Drag to move existing VD points around. Drag VD point off the VD border (shown in transparent blue color) to remove it. Click within the VD border to place a point. Press 'm' to perform 1 round of Min. Bound. Rect. Smoothing upon the VD. Press 'r' to reset the VD to new random coords. Press 'g' to toggle grid. See code comments for VD generator code's source and other implementation info.
